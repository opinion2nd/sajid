Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Brother Craft Discord bot (the "Bot") collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that data.

1. Data we store

To provide its features, the Bot stores only the minimum data required, which may include:

  • Server IDs and channel/role IDs — to save per-server configuration (welcome channels, log channels, security settings, etc.).
  • User IDs — to track leveling XP, economy balances, warnings, tickets, giveaways, reminders, and the anti-nuke whitelist.
  • Message content — processed transiently for automod and leveling. The Bot does not permanently store the text of your messages, except where you explicitly create content (e.g. a suggestion, poll, or reminder).

2. How we use data

Stored data is used solely to operate the Bot's features for the server it was collected in. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising.

3. Data retention

Data is retained for as long as the Bot remains in your server. When the Bot is removed from a server, related configuration and feature data may be deleted during routine maintenance.

4. Data removal

Server administrators can reset most stored settings via the Bot's commands. To request deletion of specific data, contact us using the details below.

5. Security

Data is stored on the infrastructure operated by the Bot's host. We take reasonable measures to protect it, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under the minimum age required to use Discord in their country. We do not knowingly collect data from such users.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data-removal requests, contact us at sajid0132569@gmail.com.
